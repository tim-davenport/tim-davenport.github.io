Hey, I'm Tim

I’m a full-stack software engineer approaching 20 years of experience in the industry. I love learning about new things and interacting with different people on projects. I'm passionate about climate change and sustainability and currently work at Octopus Hydrogen as a Senior Software Engineer. When I'm not programming I'm either playing guitar or drawing.

Climate change and sustainability

I believe climate change is real. I believe that in order for humans and the planet to endure, we must be more sustainable. That includes the way we live, work and communicate. In my view, technology has a big part to play with it.

I'm a member of Digital Humani, where I contribute towards the Reforestation as a Service (RaaS) API product as a volunteer.

I'm also a member of the ClimateAction.tech, Greentech Alliance and Work On Climate Slack communities.
